Adobe Creative Suite

In our classes, we teach using the Adobe Creative Suite. Programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, InDesign, and Dreamweaver make up our curriculum.

Apple Computing

The majority of the Graphic Design industry uses Apple Macs to design and collaborate with fellow creatives. At Rend Lake College, we have a Design Lab with 22 Apple iMacs preloaded with all of the needed software.

Job Prospects

There are many jobs in the field of Graphic Design. These include Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Layout Artist, Creative Director, and so much more.
